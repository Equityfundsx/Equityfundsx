How to Calculate Lot Consistency.

Equity Funds X 15 Mar, 2024
05 Comments 33.9K Views

Learn How To Calculate Your Lot consistency
Importance of choosing your average lot size Keeping consistency is key to succeed in the trading industry. This is the determining fact that differentiates gamblers from professional traders.
How to choose average lot size Unlike most prop firms that require lot size consistency, equity funds lets you choose your average lot size from the first trade you take and lets you use different lot sizes as long as trader does not deviate 2.5X from the AVG lot.
How to maintain lot consistency? Simple! Use your average lot which is the determined by the lot size on your first trade then multiply it by 2.5X Deviation.

Lot Consistency Equation For Max Lot use: AVG LOT * 2.5 = Max Lot. / For Min Lot Use: AVG Lot /(divide)by 2.5 = Min Lot.

Example: How to Calculate Lot Consistency
Austin Has a $600,000 Funded Account - On his first trade he uses 100 Lot on a Sell Position for GBPUSD.
His AVG LOT is 100 Lot and His Max Lot = 100 * 2.5 = which is 250 Lot. To maintain consistency he can use any lot size to 250 Lot max!
His AVG LOT is 100 Lot and His Min Lot = 100 / 2.5 = which is 40 Lot. To maintan consistency Mr Austin can use any lot size from 40 Lot to 250 Lot.

Why is following this lot consistency important at Equity Funds X Because our AMT review management needs to be comfortable to know that you are NOT controlled by fear, greed or desire at any moment of your trading journey. Which results to 85% of traders losing their live capital.

Simple Tip: For Managing a successful EFX Live Account Write down a trading plan to follow this rules or if you using a bot/EA then modify the Expert Advisor to use the lot size in between your AVG lot deviation to maintain consistency in both making profits or limiting losses.
